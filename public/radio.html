Setlist below is missing a couple tunes. Notably a couple songs by moog playwright MORT GARSON, from his Aquarius record, which itself is one record of an astrological series he did - one record for each astrological sign, each filled with mind-bending moog grooves. I stumbled on the Aquarius record while I was in Columbia South Carolina. I saw a bumper sticker on a car for a shop called JAZZ CAT RECORDS, and decided to look it up and make my way there. I was going through some dusty bins that were under the records on display, and found the record for a few dollars. It seems like that happens less these days, but maybe more so in South Carolina.
